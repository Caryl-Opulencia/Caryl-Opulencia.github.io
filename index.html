YOU ARE NOT STUPID,
NO ONE IS
Redefining intelligence

In·tel·li·gence

/inˈteləjəns/
noun

the ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills."an eminent man of great intelligence."
Definition from Oxford Languages.

“Everybody is a genius. But if you judge a fish by its ability to climb a tree, it will spend its whole life believing that it is stupid.”
-Albert Einstein

All of us, including you and I are geniuses. We shouldn't be judged based on a uniform standard. And this is what's problematic in the school system. With the current educational system, several students tend to degrade their self-worth thinking they are stupid just because they couldn’t do well in written exams or solve quadratic equations or write a 1000-word essay. Many standardized exams were created to “measure” a student’s intelligence, however we can’t just simply label someone as dumb or stupid for getting an ‘F’ in a mere pen and paper assessment, having a, b, c, or d as answers. Intelligence is in no way similar to height or weight which could easily be measured using standard scales and could be classified as underweight, normal, and obese according to one’s BMI. We shouldn't be blinded with the idea that if someone ace an exam in algebra and science, or if he or she could fluently speak in English, then he/she is intelligent or smart, otherwise, stupid.Indeed, we are all equal but it does not necessarily mean that we are the same. We are blessed with varying gifts from above.

If time will come that a fish will be judged by its ability to swim, it will live its whole life being happy and proud of itself.

But above everything elese, no matter what we are capable of, it does not and will never define our worth, our purpose, and who we are.
