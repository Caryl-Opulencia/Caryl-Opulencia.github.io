Worst Foods for your Brain

What you eat directly affects the structure and function of your brain and, ultimately, your mood. So you might as well limit eating the following foods

  • Sugary drinks - include beverages like soda, sports drinks, energy drinks and fruit juice.
  • Refined carbohydrates - include sugars and highly processed grains, such as white flour.
  • Trans fats - a type of unsaturated fat that can have a detrimental effect on brain health.
  • Highly processed - foods tend to be high in sugar, added fats and salt. Examples are fast foods which are one of our favorites.
  • Alcohol - excessive consumption of alcohol is proven to be dangerous on one's health and even on brain. Drink responsibly.
